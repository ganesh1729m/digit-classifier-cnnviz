Understanding Your Results

  • 🧠 Grad-CAM Heatmap: The heatmap shows where the model focused while making its decision.
    • Red areas → strongest influence.
    • Blue areas → least influence.
    • Use this to check if the model is “looking” at the correct strokes of your digit.
  • 📊 Probability Chart: Displays the model’s confidence (0–9).
    • The tallest bar = the final prediction.
    • Close bars indicate model uncertainty.
  • 🔍 Feature Maps: Each grid image comes from a filter inside the CNN.
    • Early layers → detect edges, corners, and basic strokes.
    • Deeper layers → capture complex digit shapes and patterns.
  • 💾 Save & Report:
    • You can save heatmaps and feature maps for further analysis.
    • If a prediction seems wrong, you can report the case for model improvement.
  • 📂 Dashboard View:
    • All past predictions are stored in your Dashboard.
    • You can revisit them, download outputs, or compare multiple results.
  • 💡 Tip: If you see repeated mistakes (e.g., confusing “3” and “5”), it’s a signal the model might need more training on those digits.
